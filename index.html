About Us

Nestled amidst the captivating landscapes of Sigiriya, Sri Lanka, GreenHaven Resorts stands as a prime example of eco-luxury hospitality. Embracing a profound commitment to sustainability, this enchanting retreat at 21120 Sigiriya offers guests a harmonious blend of modern comfort and responsible practices. Set within a private nature reserve, GreenHaven Resorts boasts a collection of unique, thatched-roof dwellings inspired by traditional Sri Lankan villages. Each dwelling is thoughtfully placed to harmonize with the natural surroundings, which include a serene lake and thriving reed beds, creating an idyllic ambiance that fosters a deep connection with nature. Here, guests can relish in the finest culinary experiences, savoring both traditional Sri Lankan delicacies and international delights, all while immersed in the abundant greenery. For those seeking a peaceful and sustainable escape, GreenHaven Resorts is a true haven in the heart of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle.
